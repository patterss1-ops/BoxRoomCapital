## Design Notes
- Value-weighted direction: buy/sell ratio uses midpoint of reported value range, not just trade count.
- Committee relevance: detects members on key oversight committees (Banking, Finance, Armed Services, Intelligence, Energy) for information asymmetry signal.
- Cluster detection: distinct members trading same direction amplifies conviction score.
- Filing lag recency: faster disclosure → fresher information → higher score.
- All four F-001 registry required detail keys emitted: `filing_lag_days`, `committee_relevance`, `net_trade_value`, `filing_count`.
