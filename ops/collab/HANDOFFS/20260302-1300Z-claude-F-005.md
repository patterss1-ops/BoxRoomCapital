## Design Notes
- Data-source agnostic: accepts pre-computed `TechnicalSnapshot` records — callers can feed from `data.provider` (existing yfinance/indicator module), TradingView webhooks, or any source. No network calls in the scorer.
- Overbought penalty: RSI > 70 scores lower than RSI 60-70 (strong momentum zone), reflecting mean-reversion risk at extremes.
- Golden/Death Cross: 50-DMA vs 200-DMA as separate pattern sub-score.
- Confidence = data completeness: 0.0 when no indicators, 1.0 when all four key indicators present.
- All four F-001 registry required detail keys emitted: `rsi14`, `above_50dma`, `above_200dma`, `volume_ratio`.
